The app we created is called Quiz2Day. Quiz2Day is a mobile learning app that we developed in partnership with The Literacy & Learning Success Centers of the Literacy Council of Lancaster-Lebanon. Our app was built to address a key challenge identified by our client, which was keeping adult learners engaged in their studies outside of the classroom during breaks, weekends, and periods when they are not enrolled.

Our application provides a simple and structured way for learners to continue practicing core academic skills. It is designed for adult students in ESL (English as a Second Language) and ABE (Adult Basic Education) programs who are working to improve English proficiency and prepare for GED-level material. Instead of long study sessions, the app focuses on short daily interactions that are easy to complete and help build consistency over time. Users select their level and complete one question per subject each day across English, Math, Science, and Social Studies. The experience is intentionally straightforward. After each question, users receive immediate feedback along with a clear explanation, helping reinforce understanding rather than just testing knowledge.

Project Goal
To support adult learners by making education:
- More accessible
- More consistent
- More engaging
- Less time-consuming

Key Features
- Daily microlearning (5–10 minutes)
- Gamified quiz-based learning
- Multiple difficulty levels (ESL / GED / mixed levels)
- Adaptive learning experience
- Broad subject support for diverse learners
